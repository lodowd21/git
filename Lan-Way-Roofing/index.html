Lan-Way Roofing Co., Inc. is a privately owned and operated business founded in 1967 by Wayne Eidson.

The core values that were established in 1967 still serve our company well. Over the last 45 years of continuous and successful business, Lan-Way Roofing Co., Inc. has evolved into a highly skilled residential roofing services company specializing in repair, maintenance, and replacement of residential, commercial and industrial roofs. We presently operate in three states, North Carolina, South Carolina, and Virginia, with our primary market area being NC and VA.

Lan-Way Roofing Gets The Job Done Right The First Time

We provide exceptional service, quality craftsmanship, and comprehensive solutions for the roofing needs of their clients. We take pride in long term professional relationships built on these values. Brownie Eidson serves as President with over 25 years of commercial and industrial roofing experience. Frank Haynes serves as Senior Project Manager with 20 years of experience in roofing, combined with an additional 10 years in commercial contracting. Brad Schaefer serves as Estimator/Sales with 13 years of experience in roofing. Michael Cobb serves as Director of Business Development with over 30 year’s experience in Business Marketing. Donna Mashburn serves as Office Manager and has been employed with the company for over 32 years. Our Field Supervisors have a combined total of over 100 year’s experience in commercial and industrial roofing.
